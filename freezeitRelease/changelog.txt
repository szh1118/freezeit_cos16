### v3.2.4SelfUse validation notes 2026-07-05
1. Self-use package target: OnePlus 13 Android 16 with existing Magisk module version 3.2.3SelfUse.
2. Includes the updated manager APK, ARM64 native service, Magisk scripts, config seeds, and rom_baseline.prop.
3. Legacy abnormal-thaw recovery now audits every 60 seconds during the first 15 minutes after daemon startup.
4. After the startup window, abnormal-thaw audits follow the configured Regular Refreeze interval instead of a hard-coded 1 hour.
5. Abnormal-thaw recovery runs while the device is in screen-off standby/doze.
6. Abnormal-thaw recovery directly requeues runnable background apps for freezing without waiting for foreground refresh.
7. No compatibility promise is made for other ROMs, devices, Android versions, or public release channels.
